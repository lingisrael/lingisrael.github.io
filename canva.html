Israeli Linguistics Olympiad


Olympiad results

The first Israeli Linguistics Olympiad took place online on May 4th.

60 students from all over Israel participated in the competition. They have successfully analyzed data of the exotic languages previously unknown to them, such as Navajo, Sahu and Hittite.





All winners will be automatically invited to the second round of selection for the Israeli team for next years International Linguistics Olympiad

IOL-2025 in Taiwan. Congratulations!
